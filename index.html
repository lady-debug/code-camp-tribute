Hunger Games

Ranked tributes

  • Katniss Everdeen: Katniss has proven that she is the most powerful tribute to ever take part in the Hunger Games.
  • Haymitch Abernathy: Most people looking at Haymitch at the time of the 74th and 75th Hunger Games would believe that he should be included among the most powerful tributes.
  • Thresh: Thresh is a contestant in the 74th Hunger Games. In the books, he is awarded a score of 9 before the Games, placing him on par with Cato (though in the films he is given a score of 8).
  • Johanna Mason: She won the 71st Hunger Games and returned to the arena in the 75th Hunger Games.
  • Finnick Odair: He won the 65th Hunger Games at the age of only 14 years old. Finnick is a skilled warrior who uses a traditional gladiator style called Retiarius
